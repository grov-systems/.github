# grov — Intelligent Systems That Act

**grov** is a professional open-source organization developing intelligent systems, starting with **ASPEN**, an AI-powered voice assistant.

---

## Projects

- **ASPEN** — AI Voice Assistant: https://github.com/grov/aspen-assistant
- **BOLT** - An AI powered word filter, fully configurable and (mostly) non-bypassable

---

## Mission

grov is committed to creating **intelligent systems that do more than just talk**. Our projects prioritize:

- **Professional-grade automation**  
- **Open-source accessibility**  
- **Customizable AI models**  
- **Local-first, cloud-optional design**
